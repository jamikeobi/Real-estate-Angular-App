Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I list my property on your website?

To list your property simply sign up as an agent. Our team will review and approve it within 24 hours.

2. What fees do you charge for listing a property?

We offer various listing packages, including a free basic package. Premium packages with additional features like enhanced visibility and marketing support are available at competitive rates. Visit our pricing page for more details.

3. How can I search for properties?

You can use the search bar on our homepage to filter properties by location, price, type, and other criteria. Advanced search options are also available for more specific queries.

4. Can I schedule a property viewing?

Yes, you can schedule a viewing directly from the property listing page by clikcing the contact agent button. Choose your preferred time, and our agent will confirm the appointment.

5. What should I do if I have a question about a listing?

If you have any questions, you can contact the listing agent directly using the contact form provided on the listing page, or you can reach out to our customer support team for assistance.

6. How do I update or remove my property listing?

You can update or remove your listing by logging into your account, going to the "My Listings" section, and selecting the appropriate option for the listing you wish to modify or delete.
